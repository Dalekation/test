Yesterday is history.
Tomorrow is a mystery,
but today is a gift!
That is why it is called the present.

Oh, Shifu. There is just news.
There is no good or bad.

No matter what you do,
that seed will grow to be a peach tree.
You may wish for an apple or an orange,
but you will get a peach.

When will you realize,
the more you take, the less you have.

There is always something more to learn.
Even for a master.

It matters not what someone is born,
but what they grow to be.

When the path you walk
always leads back to yourself,
you never get anywhere.

If you only do what you can do,
you will never be more than you are now.

One often meets his destiny on the road
he takes to avoid it.
